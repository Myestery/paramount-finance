INSURANCE POOL

A second contract will manage the insurance pool on all chains; The pools will be funded through 2% of the withdrawal taxes and 17.5% from force-withdraws. If the main contract decreases by 30% of the highest contract balance amount in the last 7 days, the insurance pool will fund the main contract.

ANTI-WHALE COOLDOWN

We have implemented a deposit&withdraw cycle and limit, which will prevent whales from targeting our protocol and manipulating it. - Deposit up to 100 AVAX every 8 hours. - Withdrawal up to 33 AVAX every 8 hours. All dividends are calculated at the moment of request and available for withdrawal 3 times a day, each 8 hour

SNOOZE OPTION

Users can extend their investment duration and increase their total return by using the “snooze” function for one or all of their active deposits users can use the “snooze” function after the end of a plan and before withdrawing it they can increase the deposit duration between 1 to 7 days the total return increases based on a compound formula
